Sound Healing Therapy Experience sound through every pore of the body and heal yourself

One of the natural forms of healing method where sound vibrations created by the singing bowls are used to bring calm within the self and restore the sense of balance between the mind, body and soul. These bowls come in different sizes and shapes and are made out of a fine proportion of sacred metals that gives a unique frequency and vibrational sound when they are hit with a wooden mallet. The soothing sound produced by these bowls helps to deepen the parasympathetic mode in a meditative state and thereby reducing stress levels and relaxing the entire body.

How does Sound Healing Therapy work?

Our nervous system plays a very important role in calming our mind and at the same time, it is very sensitive to sounds. Loud sounds can hurt and damage the delicate parts of our nervous system whereas soothing sounds will bring instant relaxation, calmness and a healing effect to the same. Our body is made of trillions of cells, which are made up of minute atoms, inside which there are wave particles constantly vibrating at a particular frequency. The vibrations created by the singing bowls are used to balance the vibrations that exist at our very core.

The harmonious sound generated by the singing bowl gives a gentle cleansing massage to the trillions of cells of the human body to release any kind of blockages and give a calming and soothing effect to the overall body.

During the therapy session, you will be asked to sleep in a comfortable position and our sound healing expert will give you the most profound experience of mystical and unexpected sounds through singing bowls.
