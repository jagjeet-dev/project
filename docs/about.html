About us

About Our Healing School

Experience and learn the ageless wisdom of Natural Healing

Are you looking for a program that focuses on natural healing experience of mind, body and spirit? We, at Rishikesh Healing School, aim to spread awareness of natural and holistic healing therapies to promote the overall health and wellbeing. Our Ayurveda, yoga and other therapies like sound healing and aroma therapy will not only help you to rejuvenate and purify your body, but will also help to relax and calm your mind. You can also learn and experience your innate ability to heal yourself through energy healing techniques like Reiki and Pranic healing. 

So come and immerse yourself with us in this holistic healing journey in Rishikesh, where the presence of spirituality in the lap of holy ganga river with a beautiful backdrop of majestic mountains makes it a perfect destination for this experience.

According to classic text of ancient India, Bhagavad Gita, the organic healing technology injected into prakrti, or material nature, is a divine gift from the Supreme source of existence. It is for this reason that pure, holistic approaches towards a healthy life, naturally help create a symbiotic relationship between physical necessities and spiritual goals. We don’t really think about our health till we get sick. However, making lifestyle changes by adopting to the natural way of living will not only keep you away from all kind of illness, but will also bring the peace and calmness to your body and mind.

Here at Rishikesh Healing school, we aim to revive the knowledge of this age-old traditional methods of natural healing like Ayurveda, Yoga, chakra healing etc. Our team of experts will not only help you experience this journey, but also provide you the knowledge so you can share it forward and thereby spread the awareness of this ageless wisdom of natural healing.

What We Offer?

Rishikesh Healing school is one place that will offer you with wide range of healing techniques.
Ayurveda Therapy

The most ancient modality of natural healing system which was considered as a way of life that includes everything related to life, food, dietary habits, balanced nutrition, lifestyle, exercises and yoga. This also includes therapies or treatments like Shirodhara, powder massage, ghee massage etc.

Yoga Therapy

A combination of yoga postures, breathing exercises and meditation techniques to either help heal any specific ailments/injuries or just to have a healthy lifestyle.

Sound Healing

Also known as sound therapy or vibrational therapy is performed using the sound and frequency to relax the brainwave patterns, slow down the respiratory rate and calm your nervous system in the parasympathetic mode.

Aroma Therapy

Blend of therapeutic essential oils extracted from natural elements like flowers, fruits, leaves etc, which is used either as topical application in massage or can be used as inhalation or water immersion. This helps in calming and relaxing the body and mind

Chakra Healing

Method to release any blocked energies from the 7 chakars (energy centers) so the Prana can flow across the body in a well-balanced way.

Reiki and Pranic Healing

Great learning opportunity of techniques where the "Prana" or "life force" is used to heal the body and mind.
