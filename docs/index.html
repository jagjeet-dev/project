Welcome to Healing School

Are you looking for a program that focuses on natural healing experience of mind, body and spirit?

Experience 7+ Years in Wellness
Certification RYS with Yoga alliance

We, at Rishikesh Healing School, aim to spread awareness of natural and holistic healing therapies to promote the overall health and wellbeing. Our Ayurveda, yoga and other therapies like sound healing and aroma therapy will not only help you to rejuvenate and purify your body, but will also help to relax and calm your mind. You can also learn and experience your innate ability to heal yourself through energy healing techniques like Reiki and Pranic healing.

So come and immerse yourself with us in this holistic healing journey in Rishikesh, where the presence of spirituality in the lap of holy ganga river with a beautiful backdrop of majestic mountains makes it a perfect destination for this experience.
